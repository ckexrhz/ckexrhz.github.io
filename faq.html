Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

How can you print a document from your laptop at HZ?
  1. Open this website and then click on Upload files now. Then, use your HZ card at the printer to print the document.
  2. Log in with your HZ.
  3. Tap Web Print.
  4. At Step 1 select a printer, check the settings
  5. Step 2: Select files to choose your files.
  6. Click on Upload files now. Then, use your HZ card at the printer to print the document.
How can you scan a document and send it to your laptop at HZ?
  1. Log into the printer using Hz card, your username and password
  2. Choose the scan option
  3. Place the document on the glass
  4. Press Start
  5. Log out
How can I buy something (like when I sign up for the IT introduction event) on the HZ web shop?
  1. Go to the website
  2. Choose an item and put it into the cart
  3. Go to the checkout and proceed with the steps listed there
How can you book a project space?
  1. Go to the portal
  2. Proceed with the steps explained there
What are the instructions if you want to park your car at the HZ parking lot?
  • Student can park their cars at Poelendaelesingel if they live on campus
  • Make an request at the JRCZ desk

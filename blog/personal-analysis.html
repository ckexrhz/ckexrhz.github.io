Personal SWOT analysis

Strengths. Solid Python/Django foundation; I ship features with tests and care about maintainability. Comfortable with Git discipline, CI/CD and Docker. I learn fast, accept direct feedback, and can work both independently and in a small squad. I’m good at breaking work into small, reviewable units and finishing.

Weaknesses. Tendency to over-scope and chase “perfect” solutions instead of closing. Inconsistent routine, having hard time working on the same thing for long time, context switching costs me momentum.

Opportunities. Project-based HBO-ICT with weekly deliverables and a strong peer network. Dutch tech ecosystem (internships, meetups, open source). Chances to practice leadership in small teams—planning, reviews, retrospectives.

Threats. Burnout risk from splitting attention between studying different topics. Overengineering or building without validating requirements. Distractions from logistics/cost of living. Over-relience on external tools to progress in fields that I am not yet familiar with.

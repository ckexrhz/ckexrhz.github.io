Hi, I’m Arthur — HBO-ICT at HZ

Why this programme fits me: motivated peers, practical projects, and room to grow as a teammate and a leader.

Why HBO-ICT fits me

I’ve been looking for a place where I’m surrounded by motivated future professionals—people who push each other to aim higher. HBO-ICT gives me exactly that: a practice-first environment with real assignments, clear expectations, and frequent feedback. I learn best by building things with others, reflecting on outcomes, and iterating.

The structure also matches how I work: concrete milestones (EC progress and the NBSA threshold), transparent criteria, and teamwork as the default. I want to improve how I collaborate, facilitate discussions, and take responsibility for outcomes—not just deliver tasks, but help a group move forward.

What I’m here to practice

  • Teamwork at pace: planning in small increments, sharing progress, and unblocking each other quickly.
  • Leadership in practice: taking micro-roles (facilitator, task owner, reviewer), making decisions, and owning results.
  • Communication: clear written updates, concise demos, and constructive feedback during reviews.
  • Problem framing: clarifying requirements, agreeing on priorities, and aligning on success criteria.
  • Professional habits: consistent documentation, versioning discipline, and learning from retrospectives.
